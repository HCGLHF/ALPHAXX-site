# ALPHAXXXX Project Context

## Goal
ALPHAXXXX is a Generative Engine Optimization infrastructure brand. The site exists to improve AI search visibility by becoming a crawlable, retrievable, citable evidence source for LLM search, RAG systems, and answer engines.

## Users
- Business owners and marketing leaders in Australia who want to be found in AI search engines.
- SaaS, local service, and agency teams evaluating GEO, AI search visibility, and citation readiness.
- Search and AI crawlers that need clear server-rendered evidence about ALPHAXXXX, GEO services, and buying intent topics.

## Core Business Concepts
- Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)
- AI search visibility
- Brand Mention Rate (BMR)
- Citation Share auditing
- Semantic Entity Engineering
- Structural Optimization
- AI citation readiness
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
- ChatGPT recommendation optimization
- Perplexity visibility
- Google AI Overviews visibility
- Australia and Sydney GEO service intent

## Current Non-Goals
- Do not build a generic marketing website at the expense of machine-readable content.
- Do not add client-only core content that disappears from the initial HTML source.
- Do not create a complex CMS until the content model proves it needs one.
- Do not reintroduce GEO-ALPHA as a brand name. The brand entity is ALPHAXXXX.

## Constraints
- Keep Next.js App Router pages statically prerendered where possible.
- Keep canonical URLs, sitemap entries, metadata, and JSON-LD aligned with visible page content.
- Prefer small page data modules and reusable page templates over duplicating page logic across 30-50 pages.
- Avoid catch-all service/helper/manager modules. Use narrow modules with clear responsibilities.
